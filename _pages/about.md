I am an Associate Professor of Mathematics at the [University of Rhode Island](https://www.uri.edu) where I started working as an Assistant Professor in 2017. In addition to helping the Department of Mathematics and Applied Mathematical Sciences, I also contribute to the Data Science program at URI. I did my PhD in Mathematics along with a MSc in Computational Finance at [Purdue University](https://www.math.purdue.edu) under the supervision of [Prof. Figueroa-López](https://sites.wustl.edu/figueroa/), where my thesis focused on constructing a microscopic Limit Order book model where the outstanding information on the non-executing side of the book is kept and the spread is allowed to move more than 1 tick. 

Othe than the PhD, I did a postdoc at [The University of Calgary](https://www.math.ucalgary.ca), where I continue working on the dynamics of the Limit Order book. Also, before my PHD, I did my Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics at [Universidad Anáhuac](https://www.anahuac.mx/mexico/EscuelasyFacultades/actuaria/) in México City, México which is the country I was born.
